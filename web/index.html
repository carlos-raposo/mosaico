Challenge your mind with Mosaico, a unique puzzle game featuring beautiful Portuguese azulejos (traditional tiles)! Arrange colorful tile pieces to recreate stunning Portuguese ceramic art. Perfect for puzzle lovers and Portuguese culture enthusiasts of all ages.

Main features:

  • extension Portuguese Tile Collections: Discover beautiful puzzles featuring traditional Portuguese azulejos (tiles) with different difficulty levels from 4x4 to 5x5 grids.
  • timer Time Challenge: Test your speed and compete for the best completion times. Each puzzle tracks your performance!
  • emoji_events Global Ranking: Compete with players worldwide! See where you stand on the leaderboard and challenge yourself to reach the top.
  • account_circle Google Sign-In: Save your progress, sync across devices, and track your achievements with Google authentication.
  • contrast Dark and Light Mode: Choose the theme that best suits your style or let the app follow your system theme.
  • translate Multilingual Support: Available in Portuguese and English, with easy language switching.
  • volume_up Sound Effects: Enjoy satisfying sound feedback as you place each tile piece, or play in silent mode.
  • celebration Victory Celebrations: Complete a puzzle and enjoy beautiful firework animations celebrating your achievement!

Perfect for puzzle enthusiasts, Portuguese culture lovers, and relaxation!
